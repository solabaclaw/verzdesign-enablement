# Verz Design - Shopify Plus Enablement Plan

> A comprehensive enablement program for Verz Design, Singapore's leading Shopify Plus partner agency

## About This Program

This enablement plan is designed specifically for **Verz Design**, a Shopify Plus partner agency based in Singapore specializing in building enterprise-grade Shopify Plus stores for brands across Asia Pacific.

As a Partner Solutions Engineer for Shopify's Asia & India region, this curriculum provides hands-on training, technical deep dives, and practical guidance to elevate your team's Shopify Plus capabilities.

## Partner Context

- **Location:** Singapore
- **Specialization:** Shopify Plus store builds
- **Market Focus:** Asia Pacific & Southeast Asia
- **Core Competencies:** E-commerce development, Plus implementations, multi-market solutions
